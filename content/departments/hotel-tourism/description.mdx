# Department of Hotel & Tourism Management

Preparing students for careers in the hospitality and tourism industry.

## Programs Offered

- **BHM** - Bachelor of Hotel Management (4 years)
- **BTTM** - Bachelor of Travel & Tourism Management (3 years)

## Industry Focus

- Hotel Operations & Management
- Food & Beverage Service
- Travel Agency Operations
- Event Management
- Tourism Marketing

## Training Facilities

- Model Kitchen Lab
- Front Office Lab
- Food Production Lab
- Tourism Simulation Center

## Career Opportunities

- Hotels & Resorts
- Airlines & Cruise Lines
- Travel Agencies
- Event Management Companies
