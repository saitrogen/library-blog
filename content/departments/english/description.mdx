# Department of English

The Department of English at Amal College has been a cornerstone of humanities education since the college's inception.

## About the Department

Our department offers comprehensive programs in English language and literature, preparing students for careers in teaching, journalism, and creative industries.

## Programs

- **BA English** - 3-year undergraduate program
- **MA English** - 2-year postgraduate program

## Faculty Specializations

- British Literature
- Indian Writing in English  
- Linguistics and Language Teaching
- American Literature

## Library Support

The library maintains an extensive collection of:
- Classic and contemporary literature
- Reference works and encyclopedias
- Language learning resources
- Literary journals and magazines
