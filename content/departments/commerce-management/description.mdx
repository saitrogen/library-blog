# Department of Commerce & Management

One of the largest departments at Amal College, offering diverse programs in commerce, accounting, and business management.

## Programs Offered

- **BCom** - Bachelor of Commerce (3 years)
- **MCom** - Master of Commerce (2 years)
- **BBA** - Bachelor of Business Administration (3 years)
- **MBA** - Master of Business Administration (2 years)

## Key Focus Areas

- Financial Accounting & Auditing
- Business Management & Strategy
- Marketing & Sales
- Human Resource Management
- Entrepreneurship Development

## Career Support

- Industry internship programs
- Campus recruitment drives
- Business plan competitions
